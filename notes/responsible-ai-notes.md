# Responsible AI Notes

CompoundIQ is an educational and research-support project. It should not be used as medical advice or as a clinical decision-making tool.

Important responsible AI points:

- Drug safety predictions require expert review and lab validation.
- Public biomedical data can be incomplete, sparse, or biased toward well-studied drugs.
- A model can miss rare or undocumented interactions.
- Results should be presented with uncertainty and limitations.
- Clear contribution labeling is important because the project was completed by a team.
